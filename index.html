27.Confectionery College – Konditorenschule

In January 1928, Bernhard Lambrecht founded the “first private school for the new art of pastry-making”. The good reputation of the school, with its Bauhaus-influenced style of confectionery, spread rapidly. Confectioners from at home and abroad came to Wolfenbüttel to become certified master pastry chefs. This unusual Wolfenbüttel institution had trained 7,300 confectioners from around the world by 1969. On December 1, 2004, the college was closed.
